Let's try seaborn.FacetGrid function.¶

We are going to explore tips dataset. The FacetGrid function allows you to explore more than 2 dimensions at a time.

Here we drew 4 subplots.

The first (0,0) subplot is tip and total_bill relationship, only for male cusomers at lunch time.
The second (0,1) subplot is tip and total_bill relationship, only for male cusomers at dinner time.
The third (1,0) subplot is tip and total_bill relationship, only for female cusomers at lunch time.
The forth (1,1) subplot is tip and total_bill relationship, only for female cusomers at dinner time.

In [7]:
tips = sns.load_dataset("tips")
g = sns.FacetGrid(tips, col="time",  row="sex")
g.map(sns.scatterplot, "total_bill", "tip")
